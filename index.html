About Our Company

Since 2000, our company has been dedicated to the design, production, and sales of luggage and various bags. We boast a modern production base spanning 36,600 square meters, over 200 experienced employees, and 8 standardized production lines, achieving a monthly production capacity of 100,000 finished bags. Our core craftsmanship team possesses over 10 years of industry experience and has long collaborated with premium suppliers such as HINOMOTO, YKK, and YIF.

We have 5 injection molding machines, 12 thermoforming machines, 2 plate machines, 60 sewing machines, and 8 assembly lines, capable of independently producing ABS/PC plates and various types of cases. We specialize in undertaking ODM/OEM custom development and also provide spot stock wholesale services.
